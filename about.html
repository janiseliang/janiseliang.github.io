GamesCrafters (2020)

Worked with team to implement a perfect player for abstract strategy games such as Tic-Tac-Toe variants and Connect Four. Learned game theory and optimized hash functions to solve games more efficiently. Implemented Alquerque, a combinatorial board game considered to be the parent of modern checkers.

Advanced Data Analysis (2019)

Worked with a group to analyze trends in opioid overdose and homelessness patterns across the US, using R and JMP. Our team won “Best Overall High School Team” in the American Statistical Association’s Public Health Data Challenge, as well as the "Statsketball" NBA Pick'em draft challenge.
